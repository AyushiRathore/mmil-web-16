ABOUT US

Microsoft Mobile Innovation Lab provides a platform for research community to collaborate on mobile application, research and development. This facilitates the faculty as well as students to develop potential mobile application to touch millions of mobile users. We believe in saying no to thousands of projects, so that we can really focus on the few that are truly important and meaningful to us.

The Microsoft Mobile Innovation Lab is a passionate team that makes ideas a reality. By adopting Agile, User-Centric, and Design Thinking practices, we solve problems and accelerate the delivery of mobile application that improve and enrich all lives worldwide.
